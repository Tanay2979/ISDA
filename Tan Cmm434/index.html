ISDA was founded by a group of students who are passionate about Data Analytics and is led by our faculty mentor Dr. Zareef Mohammed.


The ISDA’s mission is to focus on career development and help equip students with the right skills and tools that will enable them to apply relevant analytics-driven business solutions. In pursuit of this goal, we will provide hands-on experience in business analytics through, discussion, events, program simulations, and guest speakers.


As Carly Fiorina rightly said, “The goal is to transform data into information, and information into insight.” We hope to achieve that by making data easily accessible and understandable so that organizations can make the best use of the information provided to them as data is only valuable if it is used to make better decisions.
